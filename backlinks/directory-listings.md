## Long description

> Heritage Springs Lakes Paving Co & Pergolas is Heritage Springs Shire's specialist decking, pergola and outdoor-living contractor. We design and build across the Officer growth corridor (Officer, Heritage Springs Lakes, Officer, Heritage Springs Lakes, Lakeside, Officer East) and the bushfire-prone Heritage Springs Hills (Cockatoo, Emerald, Officer Upper, Officer, Gembrook).
>
> Five formats: timber decking ($250–$550/m²) in treated pine, merbau, spotted gum, blackbutt or jarrah; composite decking ($450–$650/m²) in Trex, Modwood, Ekodeck or Futurewood for zero-maintenance 25+ year service life; pergolas and verandas ($4K–$25K) in open-frame, gabled-roof, flat-roof or louvred-aluminium styles; alfresco and outdoor kitchens ($8K–$80K) from BBQ-station through to fully enclosed entertaining pavilions; and deck restoration ($50–$120/m²) — sand, repair, re-oil for aged hardwood decks that have another 10–15 years of life.
>
> BAL-rated bushfire decking is our regular weekly work. Cockatoo, Emerald, Officer Upper, Officer and Gembrook all sit in the Bushfire Management Overlay — every property has a BAL rating (typically 12.5–29, some 40 and FZ) and every new deck must meet AS 3959 to that rating. We work with two Heritage Springs-region accredited building surveyors regularly and stage the inspection at the right project point so it never blocks the build.
>
> NCC 2022 compliant by default. VBA-registered. Reactive-clay-engineered footings — 1.2m concrete pads or screw piles on Class M and H sites (most of Heritage Springs Shire). 7-year structural warranty plus 12-month defects warranty. ABN 91 664 546 061.

> Free on-site 60-minute design consultations across Heritage Springs Shire. Fixed-price written quote within 7 days of consultation.
